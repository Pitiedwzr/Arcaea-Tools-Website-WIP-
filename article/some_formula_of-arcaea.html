Some formula of arcaea

This article will show the formula of singel PTT (Playing Rating), world mode step and PTT.

Singel PTT (Playing Rating)

The singel PTT depends on your play score:

When the score >= 10000000(Pure Memory), the singel PTT = Chart Constant + 2 ;

When the score between 9800000 and 9999999, the singel PTT = Chart Constant + 1 + ( score - 9800000 ) / 200000 ;

When the score <= 9800000, the singel PTT = Chart Constant + ( score - 9500000 ) / 300000 ;

If you want to check the chart constant, you can go to this website.

PTT

PTT's value depends on "Best 30" and "Recent 10".

The best 30 means best top 30 singel PTT (Playing Rating) you got, they can't be reduced, and the score can only appear once.

The recent 10 means the best recent 10 singel PTT in recent 30 singel PTT (Playing Rating) you got, they can be reduced, and the score also can only appear once.(This value usually make your PTT change.)

The formula of PTT is:

PTT = ( Best 30 + Recent 10 ) / 40
